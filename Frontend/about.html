About COD-DATA

COD-DATA stands for Cybersecurity Oversight & Defense. We build tools that help security consultants and internal teams perform network audits, track asset-specific best practices, and aggregate vulnerability scan results.

Our platform is designed for professionals who need a single place to manage audits across diverse infrastructure—servers, switches, firewalls, and more. Each asset type gets its own checklist, so you can ensure consistent, thorough coverage without manual overhead.

COD-DATA is hosted at cod-data.com. We're committed to keeping your audit data secure and under your control.

Our focus

  • Network audits

    Asset-based checklists for infrastructure

  • Vulnerability tracking

    Aggregate and prioritize scan results

  • Best practices

    Type-specific guidance per asset

  • Reporting

    Audit status and compliance visibility
